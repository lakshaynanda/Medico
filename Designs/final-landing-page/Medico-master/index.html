About Us

Medico provides accurate and independent information about a lot of prescription drugs, over-the-counter medicines and natural products. It is the easiest way to lookup drug information, identify pills, check interactions and set up your own personal medication records.


In this you can interact with MedBot to find out the possible diseases you concern about and it will provide with possible medicines for it. If you intend to get professional help, the site also provides chat application with the doctors throughout the county where you can discuss about your symptoms of diseases and get help for it.


The site provides with personalized timeline where your history of consulting, progress reports, previous medical issues and contact details of the doctors would be provided for ease of use.


MEDBOT

Medico provides with an AI powered MedBot which helps, guide and directs you to find out the possible disease you concern about, also providing the possible remedy for it.

Medbot acts as your helping assistant, from telling you what is the disease from the symptoms to guiding you which medicines to take.
